ABOUT US

Who are we?



Team Arcis is the official Aero design Team of Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering, Bangalore. The team was founded in 2012 by three passionate Aeromodelling enthusiasts, Ankit Jain, Ribhu Taneja and Anuj Sharma.

We have been competing at the SAE International Aerodesign Challenge since 2013. The Team is ranked #1 among participating Indian teams since 2015. Our passion for aeromodelling can be seen in our work, From conceptualizing the design to fabricating, assembling, and finally flying our aircraft.

Arcis is the Sanskrit word for “Enlightenment” and we believe in an enlightened approach to aeromodelling. We rigorously study existing designs and come up with new innovations to advance the team and the science of aeromodelling .




SPONSORS

Solidworks ,a subsidiary of Dassault Systemes, is the official sponsor of Team Arcis. We use Solidworks for design, assembly, and simulation of our RC airplane.

 


ANSYS develops Engineering simulation software. ANSYS is the official Sponsor of Team Arcis. We use ANSYS for Stress testing, CFD and CFX.
